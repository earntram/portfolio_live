Math Quiz

This is an app to test your math skills. It will ask you five questions, telling you whether your answer was correct or incorrect, while keeping score of the ones answered correctly.

This app primary use is in a geometry classroom as an assessment for geometry students to see if they are ready to move onto the next content area.

The technology used was HTML, CSS, JavaScript, repl.it, and GitHub
